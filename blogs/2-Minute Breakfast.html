Why Skipping Breakfast is Costing You More Than You Think

Skipping breakfast isn't just missing a meal. It affects your entire day.

  • Lower concentration and focus in the morning
  • Increased cravings and overeating later in the day
  • Slower metabolism over time
  • Energy crashes that lead to sugary snacks and caffeine

The few minutes you save by skipping breakfast often cost you hours of lost productivity.
