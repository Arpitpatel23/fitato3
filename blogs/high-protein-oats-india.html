Protein is the building block your body needs — whether you're hitting the gym, running in the morning, or just trying to stay energetic through a demanding workday.

Most standard oats available in India deliver only 5–7g of protein per serving. That's barely enough to support muscle recovery, let alone help you power through the day.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are high protein oats good for weight loss?

Yes. Higher protein content increases satiety, helping reduce unnecessary snacking.

How much protein should breakfast contain?

Most nutrition experts recommend 20–30g protein for breakfast for optimal energy and muscle recovery.

Yes. Oats provide fiber, complex carbohydrates, and when fortified with protein, they become a balanced meal for active lifestyles.
