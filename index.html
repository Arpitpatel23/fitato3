Brand Story

Why Fitato started

It started in a kitchen in Indore. Like most good ideas, Fitato wasn't born in a boardroom — it was born out of frustration. Skipped breakfasts, bland protein shakes, and the constant trade-off between eating healthy and actually enjoying what you eat. We kept asking the same question: why does nutritious have to mean boring? So we started experimenting. Small batches, honest ingredients, real flavours — made right at home. We wanted something that a student rushing to college, a professional on a 9am call, or someone deep in their fitness journey could all grab and actually look forward to. That's how Drinkable Oats came to life. High in protein. Packed with goodness. Available in flavours that don't feel like a compromise — Chocolate, Strawberry, Mango, Mocha, Rabdi, Chocolate & dryfruit etc. Fitato is for people who refuse to choose between a busy life and a healthy one. From our home in Indore — to yours. 🌾
